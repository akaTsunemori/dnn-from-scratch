Â§DNN From Scratch - Experiments

The entry point for running two experiments:

  • RSSI Experiment: A regression experiment that maps Received Signal Strength Indicator (RSSI) to geographical coordinates (X, Y).
  • MNIST Experiment: A classification experiment that involves the MNIST dataset, focusing on digit recognition, a well-known classification problem.

The experiments are executed with a fixed random seed to ensure reproducibility across different runs. The main function invokes the experiment logic defined in the rssi_experiment and mnist_experiment modules.
